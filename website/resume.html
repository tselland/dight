Experience

I have been working in a corporate environment since I was 16. Here are some of the things I have picked up. This page is intended to share my feelings about each company and role. To see my formal resume, use the link below.

View fully-formatted resume

Business Career Center - Marriott School of Management

I started as an assistant to Reid Grawe, the placement counselor for the Information Systems program. After 2.5 years, I ended up as the team lead for all technology projects for the Business Career Center. It was a great experience to be in the know about job and internship opportunities, and to help my classmates acheive their employment dreams.

Workday

As an Application Developer Intern, I had the opportunity to work with a great team of people. I truly believe that the people you surround yourself with are key to being happy. I loved my co-workers and I look forward to joining my Workday team full-time in July 2016

USAA

I can't say enough positive things about my time as an intern at USAA. Serving the members of the United States Armed Forces is an experience I will never forget. While at USAA, I had the opportunity to participate in a mock "Zero-day PT" where you are treated like a brand new cadet at bootcamp. Hundreds of push-ups and a few miles later, I gained a deeper appreciation for our servicemen and women.

Signet Mortgage Corporation

My first office job was working for my dad at Signet. As the founder and president of this little Mortgage company, my dad gave me a lot of responsibilities, many of which I wasn't qualified for. I ran the books for a while, served as the "tech guy" and developed custom reports for government compliance reporting. It was a great opportunity to get involved in multiple facets of a company and has shaped my understanding of how a business operates.
