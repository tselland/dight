About Me

I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area, in a town nestled in one of the inland valleys.

I'm interested in biking, volleyball, and pretty much every other sport.

I appreciate good architecture and design (I recently finished The Design of Everyday Things).

There is never enough time in the day for me to accomplish everything I want to do.

I love animals, but I have a special place in my heart for Border Collies. I have no tolerance for dumb dogs now that I have experienced what it is like to have extremely intelligent dogs.
