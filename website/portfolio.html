BCC Tableau Analytics

The Business Career Center in the Marriott School of Management has recently undergone a shift to focus on using data to help students find the right internships and jobs after graduation. As an employee in the BCC, I was able to lead a team to develop analytics dashboards for the directors of placement for each of the various programs in the business school. The intent is to provide these placement directors with information about trends and indicators that will help them guide the students to achieve their dreams.
