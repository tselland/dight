Adventures

Adventures

I love to explore the world around me. I'm always looking for opportunities to get outside. Here are just a few of my most recent excrusions.

Cycling

My most recent adventure has been road biking. My wife and I have enjoyed mountain biking in the past, but my brother-in-law infected our family with the cycling bug when he began riding back in 2014. Slowly, the rest of our family has invested in road bikes and we have enjoyed riding around Utah and Northern California. In 2016, I plan to complete the ride around Lake Tahoe, a 72 mile ride.

Carribean Cruise

In early 2016, my family spent a week on a Caribbean cruise. It was amazing to get away from the Utah cold and enjoy being pampered aboard the "Freedom of the Seas". We ran into some hiccups with some Haitian protesters, and some uncooperative weather, but spending a week with no internet proved to be a much needed respite.

Exploring Utah

We have been doing our best to take advantage of all the outdoor activities Utah has to offer. We recently took a long adventure to the Fifth Water Hot Springs. Roundtrip, It took 10 miles of cross-country skiing and 5 miles of hiking to a smelly hot tub... But the atmosphere and friends made it worth it.
